1. Introduction
Welcome to studyAir! This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your information.
studyAir is a free mobile application designed for students and adult learners to increase their knowledge through quizzes and learning modules. To keep our app free, we show advertisements.
2. Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.
This means:
We do not require an account, so we do not collect usernames, email addresses, or passwords.
We do not collect profile data like display names or pictures.
All quiz scores and progress are stored locally only on your device. We do not have access to this data.
We do not use third-party analytics or advertising networks that collect personal identifiers.
3. Advertising in studyAir
Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob.This data is used to serve generic ads and is not shared with us or tied to your identity.
We have no access to or control over the data collected by these advertisers.
4. Data Security
Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no risk of it being compromised by us.
5. Your Privacy Rights
You have full control over your data because we don't collect it. Any learning progress is stored solely on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.
6. Children's Privacy
Our app is safe for all users, including children, because it does not collect any personal information.
7. Changes to This Policy
If we change our data practices in the future and begin collecting information, we will update this policy, post it in the app, and change the "Last Updated" date.
